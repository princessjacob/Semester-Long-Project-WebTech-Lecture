PROTOCOLS

Protocols are communication language between network devices and it is sometimes indicates to as an access method. TCP/IP or Transmission Control Protocol/Internet Protocol is one common protocols used in Windows. It is a routable, non-proprietary protocol that‘s the predominant Windows network protocol. It‘s supported by all versions of Windows and most other non-Microsoft operating systems. TCP/IP is also the protocol of the Internet.The Internet protocol was from research and development in 1960's by the Defense Advanced Research Projects Agency (DARPA).
