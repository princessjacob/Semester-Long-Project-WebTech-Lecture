INTERNET SERVICE PROVIDER

ISP or Internet Service Provider is used by smaller companies and individuals that provide connectivity to the internet. The first ISP was introduced in 1974 by Telnet, the first commercial version of ARPANET (Advanced Research Projects Agency Network). The first ISP's were established in 1989 in Australia and US.
